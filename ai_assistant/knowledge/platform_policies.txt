Platform Policies for CarRentalPro (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

1. Cancellation Policy:
- Users can cancel their bookings up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund.
- Cancellations within 24 hours of pickup will incur a 20% penalty fee.
- No-shows without cancellation will be charged the full amount.

2. Driver / Nominee Policy:
- In "With Driver" bookings, the driver is assigned by CarRentalPro and their details are shared via email before pickup.
- In "Rental Only", the person who booked the car must be the primary driver and provide driving license details. They can add a nominee driver, but must be present at pickup.

3. Car Listings (Owners):
- Car owners can list their cars for rental or sale.
- All listings must be approved by the Admin before they appear on the platform.
- The Admin reviews verification documents like RC, insurance, and car photos.

4. Parcel Delivery:
- Users can request a parcel delivery by filling out sender and receiver details.
- Drivers can accept or reject parcel requests.
- Once accepted, the sender receives a 12-digit QR code.
- To successfully deliver a parcel, the driver must ask the receiver for a 4-digit OTP.

5. Payment & Security:
- Security deposits must be paid during booking for "Rental Only" cars.
- Deposits are refunded within 24 hours of dropping off the car in good condition.
- Any damages incurred during rental will be deducted from the security deposit.
