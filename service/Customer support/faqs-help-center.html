Knowledge Base

Instant clarity on our most frequently asked logistical, billing, and operational questions.

How do I initiate a luxury reservation?

Navigate to the 'Book a Car' section, select your preferred vehicle class (Sedan, SUV, or Electric), and follow the prompts to configure your rental duration and protection plan.

What documentation is required for vehicle release?

We require a high-resolution scan of your valid driver's license alongside a government-issued identification or passport. For doorstep delivery, these must be verified 2 hours prior to arrival.

Which financial instruments are accepted?

We settle payments via all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), premium debit cards, and encrypted digital wallets for local transactions.

What is the typical refund cycle?

Logistical processing is initiated immediately upon return. Your financial institution will typically reflect the credit within 3 to 5 business standard cycles.

Can I modify my reservation mid-trip?

Extensions and modifications are supported via our real-time concierge. Please contact support at least 6 hours before your scheduled return to ensure vehicle availability.

What does the premium protection plan cover?

Beyond standard liability, our premium plan covers collision damage, theft protection, and 24/7 roadside recovery with zero deductible for qualified incidents.
